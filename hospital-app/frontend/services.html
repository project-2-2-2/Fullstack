Our Medical Services

We provide a wide range of medical services to meet your healthcare needs. Our departments are equipped with the latest technology and staffed by expert medical professionals.

General Medicine

Comprehensive health checkups, diagnosis, and treatment for a variety of common illnesses.

Cardiology

Advanced heart care, including ECG, stress tests, and specialized treatments for cardiac conditions.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, including vaccinations and growth monitoring.

Surgery

Modern surgical procedures across multiple specialties, with a focus on minimally invasive techniques.

Pharmacy

24/7 on-site pharmacy providing a wide range of medications and medical supplies.

Diagnostics

State-of-the-art laboratory and imaging services for accurate and timely diagnosis.
